bright wedding edit

By homework assignment
Published on July 6, 2024

i recently saw a video from a professional wedding photographer of how he proposed to edit photos to make them super-sellers. well I found that he mainly used three ideas:

- negative vignette without loss of saturation

- split-toning to increase color contrast

- some overall vibrance and exposure increase while keeping good skin color

i thought i can do that too. so here are some images from the only wedding i ever shot in three modes: raw, standard edit and bright wedding edit.
