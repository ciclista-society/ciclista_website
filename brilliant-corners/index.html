brilliant corners

By homework assignment
Published on May 15, 2024

or how i learned to enjoy minimalism in contemporary greek architecture.

during our recent vacation we stayed at the agapi beach hotel. at first the clean minimalist architecture did not resonate very strongly with me but after some time i realized that the interaction between light and the simple geometrical shapes is striking. also the contrast between the bold clean surfaces and the beautiful flowers in the garden is a joy to behold.
